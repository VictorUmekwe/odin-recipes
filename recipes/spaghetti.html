Baked Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Beef and Onions
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Butter

How To Prepare Baked Spaghetti

  1. Boil and drain the spaghetti.
  2. Cook the beef and onions together, then drain the excess oil.
  3. Add the sauce and salt.Whisk the eggs, Parmesan, and butter in seperate bowl.
  4. Toss the spaghetti in the Permesan mixtue.
  5. Layer the ingredients in a prepared baking dish according to detailed recipe.
  6. Cover and bake for 40 minutes.Sprinkle with mozzarella, then keep baking until the cheese is melted.
